AI/ML Engineer

About the Role
We're looking for an AI/ML Engineer to help build and scale our machine learning systems, from research to production.

Responsibilities
- Design and train deep learning models for computer vision and/or NLP tasks
- Run comparative experiments and benchmark model performance against existing baselines
- Work closely with the engineering team to move models from research to production
- Document findings and present results to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Stay current with the latest research and apply relevant techniques to ongoing projects

Requirements
- Strong programming skills in Python
- Hands-on experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Background in computer vision, NLP, or image processing
- Experience with Scikit-learn, OpenCV, or similar libraries
- Prior exposure to research work, publications, or benchmarking studies is a plus
- Excellent communication skills and ability to work in a collaborative team environment
- M.Tech/B.Tech in Computer Science, AI, or related field
